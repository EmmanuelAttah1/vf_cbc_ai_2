expand_more

Your Answer: I'm not familiar with that.

Correct Answer: Atomic design is a methodology for creating design systems. It breaks down interfaces into five distinct levels: atoms, molecules, organisms, templates, and pages.

Explanation: This hierarchical structure helps build interfaces systematically, from the smallest elements (atoms like buttons) to complex page layouts.
